Mel's Cookies

Only takes you 20 minutes and makes 9 delicious cookies!

Ingrediants:

  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened

  • 1/4 cup packed dark brown sugar

  • 2 tbsp granulted sugar

  • 1/2 tsp pure vanilla extract

  • 1/4 tsp salt

  • 1 large egg yolk (yes...just the yolk)

  • 1/4 tsp baking soda

  • 1/2 cup + 2 tbsp all-purpose flour

  • 2/3 cup chocolate chunks (or choco chips - whatever you prefer!)

So how do we do it?

  1. Beat together the butter, brown sugar, granulted sugar and vanilla extract until it looks smooth and fluffy - it may even resemble damp sand!

  2. Mix in the egg yolk until it is evenly combined

  3. Mix in the baking soda and salt

  4. Add the flour and stir until evenly combined

  5. Fold in the chocolate chunks

  6. Divide into equal portions and roll into balls. I usually get around 9 but it depends on how big you like your balls (cookies). Spread them apart on a baking tray lined with greaseproof paper.

  7. Bake for 9-11 minutes at 180 degrees C.

  8. Bake until golden brown and puffed. They should still be soft in the middle. Transfer to a wire rack to cool.

Now Relax and Enjoy a Mighty plateful!
