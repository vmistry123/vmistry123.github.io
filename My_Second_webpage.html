Lemon Drizzle Cake

Takes around 45 minutes and is well worth the wait!!! Serves 6 people - or 5 people and you get 2 slices!

  • 70g softened unsalted butter

  • 120g caster sugar

  • 2 medium eggs

  • 140g self-raising flour

  • 1 tsp baking powder

  • Zest of 1 lemon

  • 1 tbsp lemon curd

  • 2 tbsp full-fat milk

    • For the topping

  • 30g granulted sugar

  • Juice from 1 lemon

So how do we do it?

    To make the cake:

  1. Beat together the butter and suagr until pale, light and fluffy.

  2. Add the eggs and mix.

  3. Add the flour, baking powder, lemon zest, lemon curd, and milk. Mix with a wooden spoon.

  4. Grease and line a baking tin. Pour the mixture into the tin. Preheat the oven to 160 degrees C.

  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until a skewer comes out clean. Leave in the tin to cool.

    6. To make the drizzle:

  6. Mix the sugar and lemon juice together.

  7. Pour it onto the cake whilst it is still hot and in the tin.

  8. Remove from the tin once it is cooled.

Now Relax and Enjoy a Mighty Slice!
