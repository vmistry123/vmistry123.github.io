No-bake Biscoff Cheesecake

Requires 20 minutes or preparing - and 3 hours of setting - sorry! Serves 6 people

Ingrediants:

    For the base:

  • 200g Biscoff biscuits, cruched!

  • 80g unsalted butter, melted

    • For the cheesecake mixture:

  • 300ml double cream

  • 400g full-fat cream cheese

  • 200g Biscoff spread

    • For the Biscoff topping (optional):

  • 150g Biscoff spread, melted

  • 50g Biscoff biscuits, crushed!

So how do we do it?

    To make the base:

  1. Combine the 'Base ingrediants' and press into a tin. Chill in the dridge.

    2. To make the cheesecake mixture:

  2. Meanwhile, whisk the 'Cheesecake mixture ingrediants' in a bowl until it is very thick - until it slightly resembles ice cream!

  3. Smooth the mixture onto the biscuit base. Let it sit in the fridge for 3 hours.

    4. To make the topping:

  4. When ready to serve, melt teh biscoff spread gently in a pan, then pour over the cheesecake mixture.

  5. Smooth it out gently with a spoon, until the top is completely covere.d

  6. Sprinkle the remaining biscoff biscuit crumbs around the edges - or go crazy and sprinkl it everywhere!

  7. Return it to the fridge for another hour to let the topping set.

Now Relax and Enjoy a Mighty Slice!
